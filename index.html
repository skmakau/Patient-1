Steadi-Three Patient Evaluation: Patient 1

Intention Tremor

Tremor Scale

0 = Normal
1 = Slight (amplitude <0.5 cm). May be intermittent.
2 = Moderate amplitude (0.5-1 cm). May be intermittent.
3 = Marked amplitude (1-2 cm).
4 = Severe amplitude (>2 cm).

Pouring

Pouring Scale

0 = Normal.
1 = More careful than a person without tremor, but no water is spilled.
2 = Spills a small amount of water (up to 10% of total amount).
3 = Spills a considerable amount of water (>10 - 50%).
4 = Unable to pour without spilling most of the water.

Drinking

Drinking Scale

0 = Normal.
1 = Slight tremor (evident when drinking from a cup).
2 = Moderate tremor (spills some liquid, but able to drink).
3 = Marked tremor (difficulty holding the cup steady, significant spills).
4 = Severe tremor (unable to drink from a cup).

Archimedes Spiral

Archimedes Spiral Scale

0 = Normal.
1 = Slightly tremulous. May cross lines occasionally.
2 = Moderately tremulous or crosses lines frequently.
3 = Accomplishes the task with great difficulty. Many errors.
4 = Unable to complete drawing.
